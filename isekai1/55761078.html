Ight, fella, I may be late to this thread, but here are some of my criticisms:

1. Ditch the opening bit, it really doesn't need to be there. Any exposition can be done by Zyzz in purgatory. Ambiguity will be a very nice tool for your protagonist's past, since they are starting anew.

2. Where's the conflict??? All this chapter does is give us some backstory and some context. Either double down on the wish fulfillment, or double down on the tragedy. Our protagonist currently has no motivation and no end goal.

I suggest this: First off, age him up to 15 so he can be considered a bit more of a man, 13 is still well in "child" territory. Second off, set up his relationships with everyone better. Let is see them together, let us see a little bit of who they are. Then, kill them all. ALL of them. Tomboy gf, grandma, best friend, dead. Tear his idyllic world from right in front of him. Lastly, have this lead directly into Natty's motivation, where he wants either revenge or justice for whomever or whatever killed his village.

3. Characterize your protagonist better. Many isekai fall into this trap, and I see this happening to yours. Give him a discrenible personality right off the bat, by showing it off as opposed to telling the audience about it. Even something akin to Jonathan Joestar would be better.

Other than that, I like the concept. Overall a pleasant read so far.

The guards standing at both sides of the southern gate leading into Ringstad were busy passing their shift kicking a stone back and forth between them. “We will have to see some proof of identity. Do you have that on you?”

>Make what you just made be chapter 3 be chapter 3 or 4
>Make chapter 2 (and possibly 3) be about him surviving in the woods or sth similar, and getting his ass beaten into the ground
>Have some mentor figure teach him how to fend for himself
>End the chapter with him performing a brutal fatality on something like a bear or a yeti

Then , write a short paragraph detailing the guard's duty, before adding more or less what you wrote.

2. As for [pump], it needs a drawback of some kind. I suggest that it increases muscle size by something like 1.2x or 1.5x to remain anatomically reasonable (2x or 2.5x should be proper for a villain, though), and that it works by forcing his body to exert his muscles to their maximum capacity.
